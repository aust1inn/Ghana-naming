Akan Naming

A web application that takes a user's birthday and
determines the day of the week on which they were
born, then outputs their Akan Name based on their gender.

Below is a list of the male Akan Names
Sunday: Kwasi
Monday: Kwadwo
Tuesday: Kwabena
Wednesday: Kwaku
Thursday: Yaw
Friday: Kofi
Saturday: Kwame

Below is a list of the female Akan Names
Sunday: Akosua
Monday: Adwoa
Tuesday: Abenaa
Wednesday: Akua
Thursday: Yaa
Friday: Afua
Saturday: Ama
